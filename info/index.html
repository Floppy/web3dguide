This page is a resource for technical information on various Web3D systems. We have links to all the current specifications, and also a few possible future ones. There are some extra reference materials, which may be useful to developers, and also links to various FAQ pages, including our own local one.

FAQs
Floppy's VRML FAQ
Answers to the questions I get asked regularly.
Official VRML FAQ
The official FAQ for the VRML language.
comp.lang.vrml FAQ
The FAQ from the USENET newsgroup comp.lang.vrml. Supplements the official FAQ above.
X3D FAQ
A FAQ dealing with X3D, the next generation of VRML.

The final draft of the proposed amendment to the VRML97 specification.
EAI Final Draft
The final draft of the EAI (External Authoring Interface) specification.

The draft spec of X3D, the next generation of VRML.
X3D Encoding Specification
The draft of the X3D encoding specification, which explains how X3D is encoded into VRML97 and XML formats.
Web3D Consortium Working Group Specifications
H-Anim 2001 Draft
The draft of the newest version of H-Anim, the method for representing humanoid figures in VRML.
GeoVRML 1.0
GeoVRML is a recommended practice for representing geographical data in VRML.
Reference Material
VRML 2.0 Cheat Sheet Postscript
A two-page document with the syntax of every node, and a summary of other language elements. Absolutely excellent to have sitting next to your computer when developing VRML.

VRML97 ECMAScript Objects Cheat Sheet US Letter Postscript
Another essential two-page document summarising the VRML97 ECMAScript objects, their functions and their properties.
